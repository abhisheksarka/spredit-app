The Rules of Spredit

Publish any information you want, a photo, question, link, etc in the form of snippets.

It is spread to people around you real time.

Receivers of the snippet can choose to spread it to people around them or contain it.

Health of a snippet increases or decreases based on how people respond to it.

Spread maps tell you how far your snippet has spread.


Here the whole world is your audience, so be nice :)
