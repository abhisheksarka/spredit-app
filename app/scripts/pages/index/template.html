Rules of Spredit

Publish a photo, question, link, etc in the form of snippets.

It is spread to people near you real time.

Receivers of the snippet can choose to spread it to people near them or contain it.

This is how photos, links or any snippet keeps spreading.

Uninteresting snippets get contained and are eventually killed.

Spread maps tell you how far your snippet has spread.


Here the whole world is your audience, be nice :)
